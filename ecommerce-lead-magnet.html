Revenue Leak #2: Weak Upsells

The One-Click Wonder Problem

Your customers have their credit cards out. They just bought. This is the perfect moment to increase order value.

Why Most Stores Miss This

  • Shopify's native checkout is limited
  • Fear of "ruining" the sale
  • No idea what to offer as upsells
  • Technical complexity seems overwhelming
  • Previous bad experiences with pushy upsells

The Perfect Upsell Flow

  1. Order Bump: Small complementary item (30-50% take rate)
  2. Upsell 1: Premium version or quantity discount (18-25% take rate)
  3. Upsell 2: Different but related product (10-15% take rate)
  4. Downsell: Discount if they skip upsells (5-8% take rate)
